Response expectations

I’m an indie developer and I read every message. Response times can vary, but I aim to reply as soon as possible.

If your issue is urgent, put URGENT in the subject line.

Troubleshooting checklist

1) Restart the app • 2) Reboot your device • 3) Update to the latest iOS version • 4) Reinstall the app • 5) Try again on a stable network (if applicable).

If the issue persists, email support with screenshots (if possible).

FAQ

Does Spin Tiger collect any personal data?
No. Spin Tiger does not collect, store, or share personal data. See the Privacy Policy.
Are there any subscriptions or paid features?
Not currently. The app is free with no subscriptions and no paid features at this time. Monetization may be added in the future, and it will be communicated clearly.
How can I request a feature?
Email oskarhuber27@icloud.com with your idea and why it’s useful. I prioritize features that improve real-world value and reliability.
How do I report a bug?
Please include: device model, iOS version, app version, and steps to reproduce. If you can, attach a screenshot or screen recording.
